History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of local tech enthusiasts in a community center. Its initial aim was to foster collaboration and knowledge sharing among emerging developers and IT professionals. Over the years, it rapidly grew in popularity, attracting attendees and speakers from across the globe. By 2015, TechCon had become a nationally recognized event, known for its innovative keynotes and practical workshops.

The conference has consistently evolved, adapting to the ever-changing technological landscape. From its humble beginnings focusing on web development, it expanded to cover AI, cybersecurity, blockchain, and sustainable tech, always staying true to its roots of community and innovation.
